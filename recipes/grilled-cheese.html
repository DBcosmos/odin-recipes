Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Grilled Cheese final product

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic and simple comfort food that consists of melted cheese between two slices of bread, which are toasted or grilled to a golden-brown perfection.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients
    • Take out the desired number of bread slices.
    • Slice the cheese into even layers. You can use one or a combination of cheeses for added flavor.
  2. Spread Butter
    • Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each bread slice. This will be the outer side that gets grilled.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich
    • Place the cheese evenly on the non-buttered side of one bread slice.
  4. Grill the Sandwich
    • Place the assembled sandwich on the heated surface. Grill each side for 3-4 minutes or until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
